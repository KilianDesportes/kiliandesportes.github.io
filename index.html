The Portfolio

The Portfolio is used in 5 ISS to evaluate the student through the year on their projects and realisations.
It also allows the student to present his work and take a step back on what he did and which skills he developed.
It will make the link between the student's experiences (achievement) and the skills that he needs to developed,
according to the INSA program. I choosed to make my portfolio as a website to introduce myself to web front-end.
