Training units and related projects

Middleware and Service

During this training unit, we learned the concepts of cloud computing, web microservices (via the REST API) and oneM2M architecture. The aim of taking all these tools in hand is to enable us to set up a complete installation of action automation based on the Internet of Things. This was the final topic of the training unit, with the automation (lights, windows, heating,...) of a classroom via different sensors.
