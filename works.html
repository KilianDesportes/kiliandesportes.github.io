Training units and related projects

Middleware and Service

During this training unit, we learnt the concepts of cloud computing, web microservices (via the REST API) and oneM2M architecture. The aim of taking all these tools in hand is to enable us to set up a complete installation of action automation based on the Internet of Things. This was the final topic of the training unit, with the automation (lights, windows, heating,...) of a classroom via different sensors.

Smart Devices

During this training unit, we went through all aspects of smarts devices. We started by manufacturing in sensor and then integrated it into a more complete architecture that allowed it to acquire this "smart" rating. We then used our smart device in a Lora connected environment.


See Training Unit Page >

Software and Data Engineering

During this training unit, we learned the basics of data processing. We were introduced to ontologies and web semantics, especially its strength in data formalization and for this formalization is important. We then learned how to program in R to manipulate and also represent external data.


See Training Unit Page >

Protocol and communication

During this training unit, we have been introduced to the main protocols used in wireless sensor networks (WSN). We have done a total review of MAC protocols used in WSN and have more details on SDN (Software Defined Networks), 5G and we have also been sensitized to the energy for connected objects.


See Training Unit Page >

Innovative Project

During this training unit, we had to realize an innovative project related to the internet of things. This project took place over the whole semester and we had to organize ourselves in teams of 6 to carry out this project, from the planning phase to the realization of the prototype. Our subject was the detection and free places on the INSA campus and the putting on line of this information via an application.

Innovation and Humanity

During this training unit, we were able to learn the theoretical aspects related to human psychology and also team management. These theoretical aspects allow us to project ourselves in delicate situations that we can find in companies to know how to react. We also learned how to plan an innovative project and how to follow and plan the evolution of a product thanks to the TRIZ methodology.
