About me

Who am I ?

Currently a 5th year student at INSA in Toulouse in the Innovative Smart Systems specialization and coming from Computer Science and Networks. I obtained a DUT in Computer Science in 2018 and it's after this one that I could join the INSA of Toulouse in 3rd year. Professionally, I was able to complete two internships in a company. The first one in the aeronautics sector, for C++/Qt development for a software of configuration/update of embedded test cells. The second one in a similar technical environment (C++/Qt) but this time in the nuclear field, with the aim to develop an application and a monitoring system for radiation information.


Why Innovative Smart Systems ?

I chose this speciality in fifth years because, for me, it was oriented on technologies and topics that I think are really important and promising: The Internet of Things and connected Objects, Cloud, Big Data, as well as more classic software engineering, network and electronic. As I write these lines, I did not regret at all this choice. As I expect, we are working on a lot of technologies and methods and I am sure this speciality will prepare me very well to work in a company next year.
